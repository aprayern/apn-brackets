A Prayer Notebook.NET

-- This notebook is presented to help you learn the Biblical manner in which to pray regularly each day. As you make notations, record biblical passages, and track requests and answers to prayer, it will enable you to develop an intimate relationship with God, and a journal (record) which will remind you of His faithfulness to you in your life. May it be a genuine help to you. This is my prayer. May God go with you and Bless You! Sincerely, C. Pat Lanyon..

Welcome to A Prayer Notebook

The Prayer Summary Above as Text reads:

#0 Our Father In Heaven
Is an introduction to the prayer.
We give (in this section)
links to some material to accompany your
use of a copy of " A Prayer Notebook."
The content of the introductory phrase
is 3 simple ideas:
(1) Prayer is *personal (OUR)
(2) Prayer is *family (FATHER)
(3) Prayer is *humble (IN HEAVEN) --
"I'm praying from Earth."

 #1 Hallowed Be Your Name
Hallowing God's Name in prayer is
the ABSOLUTE ESSENCE OF
PRAYING WITH POWER.
The CHARACTER AND DEEDS of the God
who asks of us such regular prayer
about such personal things
MAKES EACH PETITION REALIZABLE!
ADORATION is first acknowledging
and then thanking God for
(1) WHO HE IS. His IDENTITY.
AND for (2) WHAT HE DOES. His ACTIVITY.

#2 Your Kingdom Come
Praying the Kingdom of God comes
involves 2 BASIC CONCEPTS.
[1] Understanding 'DISPENSATIONAL'
time periods revealed in scripture. --
RIGHTLY DIVIDING THE WORD!--2 Timothy 2:15
And [2] PREPARING for God's kingdom.
It is at least FIVE fold.
(1) SALVATION, (2) EVANGELIZATION,
(3) EDUCATION, (4) OCCUPATION,
and (5) EXPECTATION.

 #3 Your Will Be Done
On Earth As It Is in Heaven
Heb 12.9 -"Shall we not much more readily be
in subjection to the Father of spirits and live?"
Submission to God is the most difficult
(on a human dimension) to actually execute.
Many concepts entail this principle.
Praying submission prayers
BECOMES (by eventual choices) submission
itself. Other scriptures guide in tandem.
These are prayers God WILL answer!

#4 Give Us This Day Our
Daily Bread
"The MOST PRAYERS TODAY petition."
The petition principle marks a distinct
change in the prayer view:
from VERTICLE -- God-ward (PRINCIPLES: ADORATION, ANTICIPATION, AND SUBMISSION) praying
to HORIZONTAL -- Man-focused (PRINCIPLES: PETITION, CONFESSION,
AND RESOLUTION) praying. This prayer is
for our true human and earthly NEEDS:
(1)Physical,(2)mental,(3)emotional,(4)social, (5)financial.

 #5 And Forgive Us Our Debts
As We Forgive Our Debtors
After salvation the soul begins a relationship
with the Spirit of God referred to in scripture as a "walk."
It is affected by deeds and thoughts.
Sin breaks fellowship with God and causes
the Spirit of God to bring us into conviction
"(6 aspects of) CONFESSION " of sins committed
after salvation of the soul restores that fellowship.

#6 And Lead Us Not
Into Temptation --
But Deliver Us From Evil.
There are 2 main parts about the principle
we're calling Resolution: Avoiding temptation,
and then suiting up in some Armor
GOD Himself has given. God does His part,
and we must do our part!
Prayer about Temptation is biblical.
Prayer will help me resist.

#7 For Yours Is the Kingdom,
the Power, and the Glory Forever. Amen.

To close His model prayer, a return to ADORATION.
Bringing glory to God is loving others.
Loving others is really praying for them
as one prays for self. Ephesians 6:18a: Praying always
with all prayer and supplication in the Spirit.
Glorifying God includes scripture memory.
It means singing also.

#8. Application. To this point, prayer has been personal from the offerer FOR the offerer. Hereafter it becomes outward with detail yet intimacy for others. Because it is detail, God is given the power to ANSWER WITH DETAIL, thus in supernatural ways.

"A Prayer Notebook" is my gift from God now being offered to you.
You may receive it as a gift, just as it was given to me from the Lord.

It is just like our gift of salvation from the Lord Jesus Christ, you, after receiving, may give it away, and it becomes more valuable to your soul, and yet you have not lost it at all!

So this "Notebook" will dramatically change your life if you will put intense effort into your composition of it, you will learn to pray, and God will answer your prayers.
